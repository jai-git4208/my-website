Our Services - The Ivelosi Ecosystem

Redefining Connectivity

We’re not just creating a network—we’re building the foundation for a truly decentralized Web3. Our services enable seamless, ISP-free communication, secure hosting, and enterprise-grade solutions powered by AI-driven peer-to-peer connectivity and blockchain authentication.

Free Connectivity

Imagine a world where your device connects seamlessly without an ISP, cutting out the middleman and eliminating unnecessary costs. Ivelosi enables direct, AI-optimized peer-to-peer communication, ensuring faster, more reliable connections using unlicensed WiFi spectrum. No contracts, no throttling—just pure, decentralized connectivity.

Decentralized Hosting

Why trust corporations with your data when you can own and control it yourself? Ivelosi lets users host websites, applications, and files on a truly decentralized cloud, ensuring censorship resistance, privacy, and security without relying on central servers. Your data remains yours—always.

Private Enterprise & Government Networks

With Ivelosi, organizations can set up their own secure, private, ISP-free networks, ensuring encrypted, high-speed, and tamper-proof communication. Whether it’s financial institutions, governments, or corporate infrastructures, Ivelosi delivers full control over data and security.

Ivelosi Mobile App – Web3 on Any Device

Why should decentralization be limited to a select few? With just one app, any smartphone instantly becomes a node in the Ivelosi network, allowing users to access a true decentralized internet without any additional hardware. Fast, secure, and fully Web3-compatible, Ivelosi’s mobile app ensures that anyone, anywhere, can join the decentralized revolution.

Ivelosi-Powered Hardware & Infrastructure

For those who demand even greater performance and security, Ivelosi offers custom-built routers and hardware designed to power the next generation of ISP-free communication. These devices are optimized for peer-to-peer networking, advanced AI-driven security, and seamless decentralized hosting, making them the ultimate upgrade for the future of connectivity.
